Overview of Governance Issues

This documentation site has been prepared by the Treasurer of the Bridgepoint Ditch Company to provide members with factual, evidence-based information regarding a series of governance failures identified over the 2022–2024 period.

The issues documented here span administrative compliance, financial controls, tax-exempt status, and basic transparency. Each section below contains a factual summary and links to supporting documents, emails, and records. No opinion is stated where fact will suffice.

This site is distributed exclusively to members of the Bridgepoint Ditch Company. Please do not share the access code or link with non-members.
