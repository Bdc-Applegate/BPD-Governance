Administrative Dissolution

Factual Summary

The Bridgepoint Ditch Company, organized as a nonprofit mutual irrigation company under Oregon law, was allowed to lapse into administrative dissolution due to failure to file required annual reports with the Oregon Secretary of State. This occurred despite the President's responsibility to maintain the company's corporate standing.

Administrative dissolution means the company temporarily lost its legal status as a corporation, exposing members to potential personal liability and invalidating certain corporate actions taken during the period of dissolution. The documents below show the state records of dissolution and the subsequent reinstatement filings.
